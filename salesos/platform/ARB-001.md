## New Measurement Standard

**Capability ROI** — قبل كل Release Train:

| Question | Standard |
|----------|----------|
| Domain Fidelity | Does it reflect a real business process? |
| Capability Reuse | How many existing capabilities does it reuse? |
| Business Impact | Does it solve a valuable problem? |
| Operational Cost | What is the cost to operate and maintain? |
| Architectural Impact | Does it require Kernel changes or contract breaks? |
