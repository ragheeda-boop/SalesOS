# Enterprise Product Council — Resolution EPC-001

## Decision

> **SalesOS enters Stage 2: Operate the Product.**

The architecture is stable. The cognitive layers are proven. The metrics are defined.

From this point forward, the only driver for product evolution is **measurable customer outcomes in production**.

---

## Enterprise Operating Loop

```
Customer Reality
        → Production Telemetry
        → Enterprise Product Scorecard (EPS)
        → Enterprise Product Council (EPC)
        → Prioritized Decisions
        → Release
        → Customer Outcome (with feedback loop)
```

---

## Release Rule

Every release must answer two questions before development begins:

1. **What customer outcome are we trying to improve?**
2. **What evidence will prove we succeeded?**

If either answer is unclear, work does not start.

A release is not considered successful until a measurable customer outcome has improved in production.

## Enterprise Product Scorecard (EPS)

| Priority | Domain | Metrics | Direction |
|----------|--------|---------|-----------|

| 2 | Business Economics | BII, Win Rate, Sales Cycle, Forecast Accuracy | Improve |

## Product Telemetry

Required before any enterprise pilot: feature adoption, time to first value, recommendation acceptance, user journey completion, workflow drop-off.

## Evidence Before Expansion (EBE)

No new capability is added until production evidence justifies it.
